Getting started
Are there any long term commitments?
None at all. PromoNavi operates on pay-as-you-go, monthly and annual plans. You can cancel your subscription at any time and you will not be billed again.
What happens when my free trial ends?
You will be charged for your monthly subscription after the free trial period is completed. If you cancel your subscription before then you will not be charged .
How do I know which plan is best for me?
Unsure which plan is right for your business? Contact sales@promonavigator.com and well help you choose the best plan based on your business goals.
