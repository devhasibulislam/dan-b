About PromoNavi

Information written by the company

PromoNavi is a digital marketing platform. It is created to help marketers, agencies, and PPC professionals save time managing PPC accounts, automate manual work, uncover possible flaws, and get profitable insights. With PromoNavi, you can automate keyword research, do a competitor analysis, automate PPC reporting, create campaigns, track ad performance, check for broken links, and solve many more everyday tasks.
